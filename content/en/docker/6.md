## docker-compose的安装
* `curl -L "https://github.com/docker/compose/releases/download/v2.3.3/docker-compose-$(uname -s)-$(uname -m)" -o /usr/local/bin/docker-compose`
* `chmod 744 /usr/local/bin/docker-compose`
> 赋予命令执行权限
* `docker-compose --version`
> 测试是否安装成功
